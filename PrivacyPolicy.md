We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our service to you.

Personal Data
While using our App, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to:

User ID and Credentials
Full Name
Phone Number
Address and Location Data
Usage Data (Electricity readings and billing history)
Usage Data
We may also collect information on how the Service is accessed and used. This Usage Data may include information such as your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g., IP address), device type, version, and other diagnostic data.

To provide and maintain the Service (Electricity billing and management)

To provide customer support
To gather analysis or valuable information so that we can improve the Service
To monitor the usage of the Service
To detect, prevent, and address technical issues

The App may request the following permissions to function correctly:

Camera: Used for scanning meter readings or QR codes.
Location: Used to identify customer areas and technician locations for efficient service delivery.

Internet: Required to communicate with our secure servers for real-time billing updates.
